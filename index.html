The Scanlon extrapolation

The headline figure, 250 years of viable extraction remaining, is an original calculation built on depletion rate projections from Scanlon et al. (2012) and CVHM2 hydrological basin modeling. Satellite GRACE data provides the empirical depletion curve that anchors the projection to observed drawdown since the 1960s.

Sea Level Rise 2100

Dataviz series modelling six major coastal cities, Ho Chi Minh City, Miami, Vancouver, Brazil, Kuwait City, and the Persian Gulf, under projected 2100 sea level rise scenarios. Built using peer-reviewed IPCC projections and city-level elevation data. Cited in US law reviews and university research on climate adaptation.
