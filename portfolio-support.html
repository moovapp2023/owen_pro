The diagnosis & the rebuild

The conversion problem was a mismatch between reader stage and CTA weight — the existing article sent cold traffic directly to a demo request. I rebuilt the funnel in three stages: a pillar article at the top, a free survey generator as the mid-funnel capture point (7% CTR from the pillar, 27% lead capture), and an ROI calculator as the bottom-funnel closer.

The ROI calculator was a sector first — no comparable tool existed in this niche. It passed independent expert scrutiny and now averages 6 minutes 9 seconds on page. Pillar article moved from position 12 to 4.
