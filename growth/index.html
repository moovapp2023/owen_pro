GTM strategy & revenue model for a broken advisory

A VC advisory for founders who couldn't raise. Big market, broken unit economics. Brought in to quantify what was wrong, identify the levers, and build a live model charting the path to profitability.

Role: Fractional growth strategist. Diagnosis, market segmentation, product strategy, revenue modelling. Model stays live with every assumption as a toggle.
3.87x LTV:CAC (from 0.64x)
$284 CAC (from $471)
$1,100 LTV (from $300)
+$43K Month-24 cash

Initial state

  • LTV:CAC at 0.64x. Cash flow negative by month 24.
  • $471 CAC against a $300 LTV product.
  • Qualification funnel unfiltered. Human time burned on calls that went nowhere.
  • No standardised product. No trust infrastructure. No lead scoring.

Solution

Mapped the acquisition funnel. Identified four fixable levers.

  • Productise the IP into a repeatable framework.
  • Rebuild authority around demonstrable expertise.
  • Automate lead qualification with a framework-based scoring form.
  • Minimal content tier for inbound signal.

Built a live model across 18 configurations. Every assumption a toggle, not a fixed input.

Outcome

LTV:CAC from 0.64x to 3.87x. CAC from $471 to $284. LTV from $300 to $1,100. Cash positive by month 24 with +$43K before overhead.

VC rejection pipeline likely underestimated. Biggest upside variable in the model.

Content & conversion, a case study

Zest is a French HR SaaS company specialising in employee engagement and social climate measurement. Brought in to improve SEO performance and build a conversion funnel around their core pillar content. Built original tools and content from scratch in a domain I had no prior expertise in.

Role: Freelance content strategist and copywriter. Full ownership of the content cluster, from keyword analysis to tool development to conversion tracking. All tools built independently using HTML/CSS/JS.
1,100+ Sessions from content
232 Demos reserved
27% Lead capture rate
6m 9s Avg. time on tool page

What was done

Inherited a pillar article ranking at position 12. Diagnosed the conversion gap: the CTA (reserve a demo) was too strong for the reader stage. Rebuilt the funnel from the reader's perspective.

Built a custom HTML tool that generates free social climate surveys based on company goals, currently pulling 7% CTR from the pillar article. The tool page captures leads at a 27% rate. Then extended the content cluster with a budget-allocation article featuring an independently-built ROI calculator, the first of its kind for this niche, which has since passed expert scrutiny.
